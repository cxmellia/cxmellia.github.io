Kim Taehyung, better known as V, is a South Korean singer who is a member
of the global sensation, BTS!

As a lead dancer, sub vocalist, and visual for BTS, he plays various roles.
Additionally, V has a Pomeranian named Yeontan,
enjoys different types of art, and is a video game enthusiast.

V called the album layover because the journey to the end of life is long.
It would be a good idea to take time for yourself to rest, to reflect,
and to create new goals. Furthermore, V, who is romantic by nature,
wanted to express gratitude to his fans (ARMYS)
around the world for their unwavering support!
